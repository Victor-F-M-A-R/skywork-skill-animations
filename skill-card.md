## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Animation guidance may be applied in ways that affect accessibility or user comfort. <br>
Mitigation: Review snippets before applying them and preserve reduced-motion handling for users who request it. <br>
Risk: Dependency recommendations may introduce unnecessary bundle size or complexity. <br>
Mitigation: Start with CSS or a small animation helper and add larger libraries only when the project needs complex motion behavior. <br>
